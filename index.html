What the app does

  • Daily check-in with a single tap
  • Check-in history stored in the app
  • If check-ins stop for 3 consecutive days, an email notification is sent to a trusted contact you choose

FAQ

Do I need an account?
You can create an account with email and password to use the app.

Does the app provide emergency services or medical advice?
No. Still Alive? is not a medical device and does not provide emergency services or real-time monitoring.

I didn’t receive the notification email.
Check spam/junk folders, verify the trusted contact email address, and ensure your device has connectivity.

Privacy

Still Alive? is designed to be privacy-first with minimal data collection and no public sharing.
